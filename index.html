About Us

Dr. Alisha Diggs, LPC-S

Welcome to a space designed for your rest, your healing, and your highest self. After 15 years of experience, while being a wife, mother, and a higher ed therapist and educator, I believe therapy should feel less like a clinical appointment and more like the long-awaited exhale you’ve been denying yourself. For the individuals navigating the weight of "doing it all," I offer a soft place to land for when things get heavy. For the couples who can't seem to connect, I provide a mirror into their relationship to help them deconstruct outside noise and deepen their intimacy and understanding. We don't just solve problems here; we curate the life and love you actually deserve in a celebratory and affirming way where your identity is honored, not just "included".
